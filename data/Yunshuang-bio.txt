Yunshuang is a Robotics Master's student at the University of Pennsylvania, 
advised by Professor Dinesh Jayaraman. Her research focuses on vision-based 
robot learning, aiming to derive optimal representations for downstream 
robotics control tasks. She holds a BS degree in Automatic Control and dual 
degrees in Mechanical Engineering from Zhejiang University in 2022, with honors
from the esteemed Chu Kochen Honors College. Yunshuang was awarded the Chiang 
Chen Overseas Graduate Scholarship to support her master's studies 
in the US.
